About Make Aid Possible

Make Aid Possible is a systems-driven humanitarian and development organization delivering sustainable, inclusive, and evidence-based impact across the Middle East and Africa, with strategic engagement in Canada. Through locally led, partnership-based solutions, we design and deliver programs that address complex challenges while strengthening systems and enabling long-term, scalable outcomes.

Building on a legacy of work dating back to the early 2000s — with structured program implementation since 2014 — the organization has evolved into a multi-country platform delivering high-impact interventions across diverse sectors. Operating through a regional hub in Jordan, we combine strategic oversight with localized implementation, maintaining a direct presence in 22 countries and extending our reach to more than 62 countries through trusted local partners.

Who We Are

Make Aid Possible plays a strategic role in advancing inclusive, sustainable, and rights-based solutions across underserved communities. Operating at the intersection of community-based programming and institutional engagement, we work closely with governments, local partners, and key stakeholders to deliver programs that are both responsive and scalable.

Through a structured, multi-country approach, our work addresses immediate needs while also strengthening systems, building institutional capacity, and enhancing long-term community resilience. We operate in conflict-affected and high-need environments, using partnership-based models that enable safe, localized, and effective program delivery.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

We design and deliver evidence-based, systems-level solutions that address complex humanitarian and development challenges at their root. Through a hybrid model of direct implementation and strategic partnerships, we work across regions with trusted partners to ensure programs are locally relevant, scalable, and sustainable.

Our Vision

To lead the evolution of humanitarian and development practice by building a globally influential, evidence-based platform that bridges research, policy, and implementation — shaping how development is designed, delivered, and sustained at scale.

Leadership & Governance

Make Aid Possible operates under a multi-level governance and leadership structure. The organization is guided by a Board of Directors, supported by Strategic Advisors and Honorary Board Members, with a dedicated regional board for the Middle East and Africa based in Jordan.

Scale and Positioning

Through its multi-country operations and extensive partnership network, Make Aid Possible contributes to development efforts at scale — positioned as a trusted partner for governments, institutions, and international stakeholders.
