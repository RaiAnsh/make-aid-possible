About Make Aid Possible

A systems-driven humanitarian and development organization delivering sustainable, inclusive, and evidence-based impact across the Middle East and Africa.

About Make Aid Possible

Make Aid Possible is a systems-driven humanitarian and development organization delivering sustainable, inclusive, and evidence-based impact across the Middle East and Africa, with strategic engagement in Canada.

Through locally led, partnership-based solutions, we design and deliver programs that address complex challenges while strengthening systems and enabling long-term, scalable outcomes.

Building on a legacy of work dating back to the early 2000s — with structured program implementation since 2014 — the organization has evolved into a multi-country platform delivering high-impact interventions across diverse sectors.

Operating through a regional hub in Jordan, we combine strategic oversight with localized implementation, maintaining a direct presence in 22 countries and extending our reach to more than 62 countries through trusted local partners.

Our programs are grounded in local needs and aligned with national priorities, ensuring coordinated, context-driven interventions that deliver sustainable development outcomes and long-term, system-level impact.

Who We Are

Make Aid Possible plays a strategic role in advancing inclusive, sustainable, and rights-based solutions across underserved communities.

Operating at the intersection of community-based programming and institutional engagement, we work closely with governments, local partners, and key stakeholders to deliver programs that are both responsive and scalable.

Through a structured, multi-country approach, our work addresses immediate needs while also strengthening systems, building institutional capacity, and enhancing long-term community resilience.

We operate in conflict-affected and high-need environments, using partnership-based models that enable safe, localized, and effective program delivery.

Our Mission

We design and deliver evidence-based, systems-level solutions that address complex humanitarian and development challenges at their root.

Through a hybrid model of direct implementation and strategic partnerships, we work across regions with trusted partners to ensure programs are locally relevant, scalable, and sustainable.

By integrating research, data, and innovation into every stage of delivery, we strengthen systems, empower communities, and deliver measurable, long-term impact.

Our Vision

To lead the evolution of humanitarian and development practice by building a globally influential, evidence-based platform that bridges research, policy, and implementation.

Through sustained, multi-country engagement and strategic partnerships, we aim to set new standards for impact, enabling communities and institutions worldwide to achieve sustainable, resilient, and inclusive development.

Our vision is centered on driving system-level transformation — shaping how development is designed, delivered, and sustained at scale.

Our Approach

Make Aid Possible delivers impact through an integrated model that combines direct implementation, strategic partnerships, and system-level engagement — ensuring that interventions are both effective in the short term and sustainable over time. Our approach focuses on strengthening systems, enabling local ownership, and driving long-term transformation across regions.

Systems-Level Focus

We address root causes by strengthening institutions, improving service delivery, and building long-term resilience.

Localization and Ownership

Data, Evidence, and Learning

Alignment with National Systems

All programs are aligned with national priorities and institutional frameworks to ensure coordinated and sustainable outcomes.

Sustainability and Scale

We design solutions that are built to last — focusing on quality, scalability, and system strengthening.

Leadership & Governance

Make Aid Possible operates under a multi-level governance and leadership structure designed to ensure accountability, transparency, and strategic direction.

The organization is guided by a Board of Directors, supported by Strategic Advisors and Honorary Board Members, bringing expertise across development, governance, and institutional leadership.

At the regional level, a dedicated board for the Middle East and Africa provides oversight to ensure alignment with regional priorities and contextual realities.

This framework ensures effective decision-making, strong oversight, and institutional integrity across all areas of work.
